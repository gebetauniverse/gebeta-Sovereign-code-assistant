# Why Gebeta Built This

## The Problem

Modern AI coding tools are powerful — but most require developers to send source code, prompts, and engineering context to hosted cloud systems.

For many teams, that creates unacceptable risk:

- **Proprietary IP** exposed to third-party AI training
- **Compliance violations** in regulated industries (fintech, healthtech, government)
- **Auditability gaps** — no record of what AI was asked or did
- **Control erosion** — AI agents with unclear permissions

Developers and founders should not have to trade privacy, control, and ownership just to benefit from AI.

## The Belief

> **AI should work inside your engineering environment — not above it.**

This means:

- **Your models stay local** — not on someone else's servers
- **Your rules stay local** — not dictated by a vendor
- **Your code stays local** — not processed externally
- **Your approvals stay human** — not automated away

## The Vision

Gebeta is building **local-first developer infrastructure** for a future where software teams can move faster without surrendering control of their intellectual property.

We believe the real competitive advantage will not be "who uses AI" but **who uses AI with control, ownership, and discipline.**

## The Solution

Gebeta Sovereign Code Assistant is not just another AI coding tool.

It is a **control system** around local AI models:

- **Policy enforcement** — commands require approval
- **Project guardrails** — coding standards enforced
- **Auditability** — actions are logged and reviewable
- **Deployment modes** — privacy or productivity, your choice
- **Team standardization** — consistent, governed AI workflows
